Our Process

At Brielle Élan, we provide a refined, tailored experience that begins with consultation and flows seamlessly into creation. Every stage is carefully designed to transform your vision into an enduring masterpiece, showcasing our commitment to precision and uncompromising excellence.

CONSULTATION PROCESS

1. Initial Inquiry:

Begin your journey with us by reaching out through our website or contact form to schedule an exclusive consultation.

2. Consultation fee:

A non-refundable premium consultation fee of #55,000 Naira is required. This investment secures your bespoke experience, guaranteeing dedicated personalization, and is applied independently of your final purchase.

3. Personal Consultation:

In-Person: Experience the luxury of a face-to-face meeting where we delve into your vision and preferences, and take precise measurements.

Virtual: Connect with us via a sophisticated video call to discuss your aspirations and requirements, with detailed measurement guides provided for accuracy.

This consultation usually lasts one hour. However, should you require more than one hour, we strongly advise you to inform your relationship manager (which we will provide) before the consultation slot is allotted to you. Please note that extensions beyond one hour will be billed accordingly.

Reschedule: Should you wish to reschedule, we require a minimum of 24 hours notice which will enable us to guarantee another slot immediately.

4. Design Proposal & Quotation:

Step into the creative journey as we shape unique design concepts and discuss fabric selections. Within 72 hours, you will receive a personalized sketch and proposal crafted from our consultation. Each consultation fee covers up to two rounds of design revisions.

5. Completion:

Upon your approval of the design and quotation, we will provide a detailed timeline for the creation of your bespoke garment, ensuring transparency and anticipation for the final masterpiece.
