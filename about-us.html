MISSION

At Brielle Élan, our mission is to craft exquisite, custom made bridal and couture pieces that honor individuality and embody timeless elegance.

We seamlessly merge traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design, transforming each client’s vision into a masterpiece defined by exceptional detail, quality, and sophistication.

VISION

Our vision is to become a global symbol of luxury couture, redefining bridal and eveningwear with unparalleled artistry, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to individuality.

About the Founder

- Oyeyemi Oyekale

Brielle Élan was founded by Oyeyemi Oyekale, a visionary designer whose passion for creating timeless couture is deeply rooted in personal meaning. The brand draws its name and inspiration from Oyeyemi’s daughter, Brielle, whose name means “strength from God.

For Oyeyemi, Brielle represents more than just a child—she is a source of strength, grace, and inspiration. Naming the brand after her was a way of weaving that strength into every design, ensuring that each piece embodies resilience, beauty, and elegance.

Long before the official launch of Brielle Élan, Oyeyemi had been designing bespoke bridal pieces privately, working closely with brides to bring their visions to life. Each gown created was a celebration of individuality, crafted with meticulous attention to detail and artistry. These quiet beginnings laid the foundation for what Brielle Élan has become today: a luxury couture house that honors women by making them feel powerful, elegant, and unforgettable.

Under Oyeyemi’s leadership, Brielle Élan continues to stand as a brand inspired by love, strengthened by faith, and dedicated to creating couture that tells a story as unique as the women who wear it.
